About

This interactive map highlights major sustainable developments in the UAE — Masdar City, The Sustainable City (Dubai), and Al Bahar Towers. These projects demonstrate innovative approaches to energy efficiency, low-carbon design, and climate adaptation.

Estidama Vision

Estidama (meaning “sustainability” in Arabic) is Abu Dhabi’s framework for sustainable design and operation. It ensures that new communities and buildings support a greener, resource-efficient UAE future.

  • 50% clean energy target by 2050
  • 20% carbon reduction in building sector by 2030
  • All new buildings to meet Pearl Rating standards
